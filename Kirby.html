Super Mario



Synopsis

This is a series about a little pink puff-ball saving his planet from the evil King Dedede. Kirby copies the abilities of his enemies, and uses their powers to help him succeed. The first game in the series was Kirby's Dream Land, which released on the Game Boy in 1992. Most recently Kirby Star Allies released on the Nintendo Switch in 2018.
