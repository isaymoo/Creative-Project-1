Legend of Zelda



Synopsis

This series is about a silent left-handed hero on a quest to save his princess from an evil sorceror. Link solves puzzles in his quest, and tries to defeat Ganon. The first game in this series was The Legend of Zelda, which released on the Nintendo Entertainment System in 1986. The most recent game is Link's Awakening, a remake of a Game Boy title, released on the Nintendo Switch in 2019.
