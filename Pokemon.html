Pokemon



Synopsis

This series is about little kids being thrown out into a world of monsters and forcing the monsters to fight each other. The games are RPG's, and are often released in groups of 2 titles each with their own exclusive monsters. The first games are Pokemon Red and Blue released on the Game Boy in 1996, and the most recent titles are Pokemon Sword and Shield released on the Nintendo Switch in 2019.
