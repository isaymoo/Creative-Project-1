Synopsis

This website shows the characters in Super Smash Bros Ultimate. It exists because of the lack of the Museum feature in the game, and I want to be able to look at these things whenever I want, and to bring it up wherever. I am starting with only seperating out the characters by the series that they originated from, and saying what the first and most recent game in each series is, and afterwards (at a different time) I will update this with more complete bios on these characters, including what game they originated in, what smash game they originated in, and their moves. I will then make a tier list of how I think of every character in the game.
